Application Process

A simple, transparent process — from finding a unit to picking up your keys.

How it works

We keep the process straightforward. Most applicants hear back within one to two business days of submitting a complete application.

  1. 1

    Browse & find a unit you love

    Start on our Apartments page to see what's currently available. Each listing shows the neighborhood, size, rent, and key amenities. If nothing is quite right today, contact us — new units open up regularly.

  2. 2

    Schedule a showing

    Call us at (216) 393-7779 or use the contact form to request a viewing. We'll confirm a time within one business day. Showings are available Monday–Saturday.

    💡 Come with questions about the building, neighbors, and anything you'd want to know before signing a lease. We'll give you honest answers.
  3. 3

    Submit your application

    Once you've seen the unit and want to move forward, we'll send you a rental application. Have the following ready to speed things up:

    • Government-issued photo ID
    • Two most recent pay stubs (or proof of income)
    • Contact info for previous landlords (last 2 years)
    • Application fee — $35 per adult applicant (covers credit & background check)
    💡 All adult occupants (18+) must complete a separate application form.
  4. 4

    Screening & approval

    We run a standard credit and background check through a third-party screening service. We review income (generally 3× monthly rent), rental history, and credit. We evaluate every application individually and do not discriminate on any protected basis.

    You'll hear from us within 1–2 business days of a complete application.

  5. 5

    Sign the lease

    If approved, we'll send a lease agreement for your review. We use a standard Ohio residential lease. Read it carefully, ask us anything, and sign when you're ready.

    💡 Leases are typically 12 months. We can discuss other terms on a case-by-case basis.
  6. 6

    Pay first month & security deposit

    Before receiving keys you'll pay the first month's rent and a security deposit equal to one month's rent. We accept certified check, money order, or bank transfer. Personal checks are accepted for ongoing rent payments.

  7. 7

    Move in — welcome home!

    We'll do a walk-through with you on move-in day, document the unit's condition together, and hand you the keys. You'll have our direct contact info for anything that comes up.

    🎉 That's it. We're glad to have you.
