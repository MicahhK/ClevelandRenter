Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often from prospective and current tenants.

Applying

You'll need a valid government-issued photo ID, your two most recent pay stubs or other proof of income, and contact information for your landlords from the past two years. There's also a $35 application fee per adult (18+) applicant, which covers the credit and background check.

We don't have a hard cutoff. We look at the full picture — income, rental history, and credit together. A lower score paired with strong income and good references may still be approved. If you have concerns, reach out before applying and we can talk through it honestly.

We generally look for a gross monthly income of at least 3× the monthly rent. For example, a $1,000/month unit requires roughly $3,000/month in gross income. Multiple roommates can combine incomes. Self-employed applicants can provide bank statements or tax returns in place of pay stubs.

Once we have a complete application — all documents and the application fee — we typically make a decision within one to two business days. Incomplete applications take longer, so have everything ready before submitting.

Pets

Pet policies vary by unit — some properties allow cats or small dogs, others do not. Each listing will note whether pets are welcome. If you have a specific pet, contact us before applying and we'll tell you which units are a good match. We do not accept aggressive breeds as defined by our insurance policy.

Yes. For pet-friendly units we charge a refundable pet deposit of $250 per pet, in addition to the standard security deposit. The pet deposit is returned at move-out provided there is no pet-related damage beyond normal wear.

Deposits & Fees

The security deposit is equal to one month's rent and is due before move-in along with the first month's rent. It is held in accordance with Ohio landlord-tenant law and returned within 30 days of move-out, minus any documented deductions for damage beyond normal wear and tear.

It varies by property. Some units include heat and/or water; others are tenant-pay-all. Each listing clearly states what's included. If you're unsure, ask before applying — we'll give you a straight answer so you can budget accurately.

The only upfront costs are the application fee ($35/adult), first month's rent, and security deposit. There are no move-in fees or administrative fees. Late rent incurs a $50 late fee after a 5-day grace period, as stated in the lease.

Lease & Living

Standard leases are 12 months. We may consider shorter terms (6 months) for a modest rent premium, subject to availability. Contact us to discuss options for your situation.

Call or text us directly at (216) 393-7779, or send an email to clevelandrenter@gmail.com. We respond to non-emergency requests within 24 hours and address emergencies (no heat, gas leak, flooding) the same day. You'll always reach a real person.

Yes, and we encourage it. We'll reach out 60–90 days before your lease ends to discuss renewal. We value long-term tenants and try to keep rent increases reasonable and predictable. We'll never blindside you with a dramatic increase.

Guests staying fewer than 14 consecutive days are fine. If someone will be living with you long-term, they need to be added to the lease and go through the standard screening process. This protects everyone and is a standard requirement under Ohio law.
